Description

The Diploma in Hotel Management – 2 years, is a detailed 2-year diploma course which offers comprehensive knowledge of hotel management to aspirants who have passed their 10th Standard and are looking to pursue a career in the Hotel Industry.

Add on Programs for B.Sc Student: Free
  • Masters Diploma in Culinary Skills
  • Diploma in Food & Beverage Service
  • Diploma in Bakery & Confectionery
  • Diploma in Front Office Management
  • Diploma in Corporate House Keeping
Note: Student can select any one of the above as per their interest.
  • 2 Years - Studying in India (Full-time)
  • Program Eligibility: 10th Pass
  • Facilities : Audio Visual Facility, Access to Library & Internet Lab
  • Faculty : Qualified and experienced faculty on a full-time basis
  • Contact Hours : 8 hours per week
  • Commencement : Admissions open throughout the year
  • For additional Information : Contact: 8881-012-012
