From The Vice Chairman’s Desk

Dear Students,

I take great pleasure in welcoming you to La Sphère School of Hotel Management. A school with an innovative approach to teach hotel management by equipping you with global knowledge and help you build a successful career in hospitality or tourism sectors.

The setting up La Sphère was like a long-drawn battle to establish an institute by developing a pedagogy that would give real meaning to the world of hospitality. Here our main purpose is to inculcate not only wisdom but also develop the overall personality of the student. We aim to make you a complete individual, confident to face the realities of the world.

La Sphère School of Hotel Management aspires to be a role model in years to come as students will experience a never before kind of learning experience which will be a mix of theory and practice.

It is said that the tourism and hospitality industries are emerging as the key drivers of growth in the services sector in India and is also attracting huge Foreign Direct Investment leading to a huge demand for skilled professionals in all areas, La Sphère School of Management is all set to churn out talented and skilled professionals to the world of hospitality.

With Best Wishes

Shivangi Yadav


Vice-Chair Person

La Sphère believes that 'Knowledge is Power' and hence, we prepare our students for a better tomorrow in an ever-changing world with the help of quality education
