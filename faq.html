About the program

How do the program work?

The programs are to provide the every student with academic qualification. The minimum requirements needed to qualify & the selection process is different for different programs. Our Assessment Board & Administrative Division will evaluate the prospective student’s application and determine eligibility. Once approved, an applicant can then decide to proceed by taking the required program of study. The Application process usually takes 2-4 weeks.

How do I apply?

Method 1

The Application form & prospectus must be requested in post with a DD/Cash of Rs 370/- (cash or DD in favor of “Ashrafi Educational Trust” payable at Mumbai, Maharashtra India)

Method 2

Online Form & Payment can be filled at www.lasphere.in/onlineapplication

Entrance Exam Fees

How much is the Entrance Exam fee?

Your Entrance Exam is totally free of cost.

Degree Authenticity and Career Opportunities

Is the degree Verifiable?

Yes, your degree is verifiable.

Is La Sphère School of Hotel Management accredited?

Yes, La Sphère has the legal authority to operate & run. It is managed by Ashrafi Educational Trust (Govt. Registered). The Institute has set out the requirements necessary to qualify for each program based upon the organization and industrial requirement.

Who will accept my Degree?

La Sphère School of Hotel Management graduates (are / can be ) accepted by companies throughout the hospitality, tourism sectors nationally and internationally. Employers know they have proven competency in the field of hotel management.

How can the La Sphère School of Hotel Management help in Job?

La Sphère has a dedicated Training & Placement cell, assuring a position in the corporate world. Our placement organization JOJO International Pvt Ltd has a very good track record of having successful placements all over the globe from years. Details of the students can be seen in training & placement pages. Also, the student once acquire a degree becomes a lifelong member (Alumni) for career counseling service free of cost at national or international level.

La Sphère believes that 'Knowledge is Power' and hence, we prepare our students for a better tomorrow in an ever-changing world with the help of quality education
