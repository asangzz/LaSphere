Eligibility and Procedure

Availability of Forms

College admission forms are available online at www.lasphere.in and can be collected in person from college campus.

Form Submition Process

Completed application form must be submitted with attested testimonials and application fees of Rs 370/- (Online/Cash/DD in favor of “Ashrafi Educational Trust” payable at Mumbai, Maharashtra India).

Eligibility Criteria

Admission is strictly based on merit. All candidates will have to clear entrance test and a personal interview round. • Scores of National and State Level Entrance exams shall be considered for selection.

A pass in 10+2 system of Senior Secondary examination or its equivalent with English as one of the subjects. Candidate must have passed English as a subject of study (core/elective/functional) in the qualifying examination.

Those appearing in 10+2 or equivalent examination can also appear in provisional basis. Provisional admission will stand cancelled if proof of having passed the qualifying examination (10+2 or its equivalent) is not submitted at the time of counseling or at the time of admission.

For Diploma Courses, 10th pass/fail student can apply.

Form Verification

The offer of admission is subject to verification of original certificates/documents at the time of admission.

Admission Terms

Candidate Applying in other Institutes

If a candidate has applied simultaneously for admission to other institutes, this fact must be stated in the application form for being considered for refund of fee deposit if any.

Provisional and Confirmed Admissions

Provisional admission shall be offered only after payment of fees and confirmed only after verification of certificates by the competent authorities.

Transfer Of Student

A candidate once admitted to the first year or semester of a program shall not be transferred during the same year or semester to any other institution.

The rule for transfer of candidates shall be applicable between unaided institutions, provided both institutions are agree, in writing (No Objection Certificate), to such transfer.

Cancellation Of Admission

If an admitted candidate cancels his/her admission by submitting an application in writing, for any reason whatsoever, including getting admission in any other institution/program (other than those which are within the admission purview of the concerned Admission Officer), he/she shall forfeit his/her candidature and reconsideration of candidature.

If during the program of studies a candidate desires to discontinue his/her studies for any reason whatsoever, he/she shall be allowed to do so by submitting an application in writing. He/she shall be issued a leaving certificate as provided in the rules.

Any application for cancellation shall not be entertained unless received in the office on or before the last date prescribed.
